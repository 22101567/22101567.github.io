DOOM Eternal

Doom Eternal is a fast-paced, first-person shooter that puts players in the boots of the legendary Doom Slayer as he battles the forces of Hell across Earth and beyond. With intense combat, powerful weapons, and fluid movement mechanics, the game delivers an adrenaline-pumping experience that pushes players to master speed, strategy, and brutal efficiency. It's a relentless fight for survival in a visually stunning and action-packed world.

Carrion

Carrion is a reverse horror game where you play as a mysterious, shape-shifting creature hunting down the humans who imprisoned you. With fluid, fast-paced movement and gruesome abilities, you navigate through a sprawling facility, growing stronger and more terrifying as you escape. It's a unique, atmospheric experience that turns the horror genre on its head. Every encounter is a blend of strategy and chaos, making each moment as thrilling as it is unsettling.

Terraria

Terraria is a 2D sandbox adventure game where players explore, build, and fight in a procedurally generated world. With a vast array of crafting options, you can gather resources, construct elaborate structures, and battle fierce enemies. The game offers endless possibilities, with cooperative multiplayer, exciting boss fights, and a deep progression system to keep you engaged for hours. Its pixel-art style and constant updates ensure there's always something new to discover.
