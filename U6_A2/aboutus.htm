About Us

At Celtec, we strive to build worlds that spark imagination, challenge the mind, and connect players across platforms and generations. Founded in 2015 by a small group of friends with a shared love for gaming and storytelling, Celtec was built on the belief that great games come from great ideas and even greater collaboration. Our talented team of designers, developers, and artists pour their passion into every detail, blending rich narratives with cutting-edge technology to deliver unforgettable experiences. Whether it’s a whimsical puzzle adventure for kids or a gripping action title for seasoned gamers, we believe in the power of games to both entertain and inspire. Rooted in the creative culture of North East Wales, we’re proud to champion in everything we do.
